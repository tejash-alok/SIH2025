Smart Crop Advisory System
for Small and Marginal Farmers

Description

The Smart Crop Advisory System helps small and marginal farmers optimize crop yields, reduce losses, and improve sustainable practices using real-time data, AI, and mobile accessibility. The system offers timely guidance on crop management, disease control, irrigation, and financial advice in an accessible way.

Core Features

  • Real-time agro-advisory: Provides farmers with instant recommendations on crop management, pest control, weather-based strategies, and nutrient application via SMS or app notifications, reducing the yield gap and increasing productivity.
    [ijsret+1]
  • AI-based disease diagnosis: Uses deep learning models (e.g., CNNs) to diagnose crop diseases from images, enabling early interventions and minimizing crop losses.
    [ijisrt+1]
  • Irrigation and fertilizer guidance: Analyzes sensor and weather data to recommend precise irrigation schedules and optimal fertilizer application, promoting sustainable use of resources.
    [uniconvergetech+1]
  • Easy access and low-tech requirements: Platforms are designed for low-bandwidth environments and ordinary smartphones, ensuring accessibility even for farmers in remote regions.
    [ijisrt]
  • Market and financial advice: Offers market price forecasts, subsidy and loan guidance, and networking capabilities to help farmers make informed financial decisions.

Benefits

  • Improved yields: Timely, relevant advice helps farmers increase productivity and reduce losses.
  • Cost savings: Precision irrigation and fertilizer use reduce unnecessary spending and environmental impact.
  • Risk mitigation: Early alerts on weather and pest threats minimize damage to crops.
  • Financial empowerment: Knowledge on subsidies, loans, and market trends aids sound decision making.
